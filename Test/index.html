Protest Against Nirmala Rape Case

Nirmala Panta was a 13 year old Nepali girl of Bhimdutta municipality of Kanchanpur, who was raped and mudered brutally in a sugarcane field near her home. It's been over 2 years but the government is still not able to deliver justice to Nirmala and her family.

  • Almost 600 cases of rape took place within the three months after the case of Nirmal Panta.
  • So the government and related authorities should take strict actions against such heinous offence.
  • The respected community leaders must raise voice against such violence.
  • All the youths should stand togeather to stop social crimes.

Nowadays people have started a social media protest with "#justicefornirmala" tag demanding for further investigation and justice for her case.
